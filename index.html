Howdy! I'm Michael Yang

I am currently a junior computer science major at Texas A&M University. I've had experiences in
web development (full-stack), Android application development, and machine learning, specifically
biometric deep learning, in both academia and industry.

Background

My first exposure to computer science was in 7th grade. At the time, I was obsessed with
the mobile hit Flappy Bird. After playing (and raging) for countless hours, it dawned on me:
I want to create my own Flappy Bird! That night, I spent hours downloading the IOS SDK kit and
blindly following a Youtube tutorial coding Flappy Bird line by line, none of which made sense
to me at the time. In the end, the application failed to compile but the seeds of my fascination
with programming were planted, launching my long computer science journey.
